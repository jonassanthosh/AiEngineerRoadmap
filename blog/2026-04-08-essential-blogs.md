## The Must-Read Blogs

### [Lilian Weng's Lil'Log](https://lilianweng.github.io/)

By Lilian Weng (OpenAI). Widely considered the best technical AI blog on the internet. Her posts are extraordinarily thorough and well-cited. Essential reading:

- [LLM Powered Autonomous Agents](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2023-06-23-agent/) — The definitive guide to building agent systems with LLMs
- [The Transformer Family v2](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2023-01-27-the-transformer-family-v2/) — Comprehensive survey of every major Transformer variant
- [Prompt Engineering](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2023-03-15-prompt-engineering/) — Deep dive into prompting techniques
- [Extrinsic Hallucinations in LLMs](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2024-07-07-hallucination/) — Understanding and mitigating hallucination
- [Reward Hacking in RLHF](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2024-11-28-reward-hacking/) — When RL goes wrong
- [Thinking Tokens: Test-Time Compute](https://lilianweng.github.io/posts/2025-05-01-thinking/) — Chain-of-thought and inference-time scaling

---

### [Jay Alammar](https://jalammar.github.io/) / [Substack](https://newsletter.languagemodels.co/)

The best visual explanations of ML concepts anywhere. If you're a visual learner, start here:

- [The Illustrated Transformer](https://jalammar.github.io/illustrated-transformer/) — The most popular Transformer explainer ever written
- [The Illustrated GPT-2](https://jalammar.github.io/illustrated-gpt2/) — How GPT-style language models work
- [The Illustrated BERT](https://jalammar.github.io/illustrated-bert/) — BERT and transfer learning in NLP
- [The Illustrated Word2Vec](https://jalammar.github.io/illustrated-word2vec/) — Word embeddings visualized
- [The Illustrated Stable Diffusion](https://jalammar.github.io/illustrated-stable-diffusion/) — How image generation works
- [The Illustrated DeepSeek-R1](https://newsletter.languagemodels.co/p/the-illustrated-deepseek-r1) — Reasoning models explained visually
- [The Illustrated GPT-OSS](https://newsletter.languagemodels.co/p/the-illustrated-gpt-oss) — OpenAI's first open model in six years

---

### [Andrej Karpathy](https://karpathy.ai/)

Founding member of OpenAI, former Director of AI at Tesla. Karpathy writes rarely but every post is a classic:

- [The Unreasonable Effectiveness of RNNs](https://karpathy.github.io/2015/05/21/rnn-effectiveness/) — The blog post that got a generation into deep learning
- [A Recipe for Training Neural Networks](https://karpathy.github.io/2019/04/25/recipe/) — Practical debugging and training advice
- [nanoGPT](https://github.com/karpathy/nanoGPT) — The simplest, most readable GPT implementation
- [Let's build GPT from scratch](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kCc8FmEb1nY) — 2-hour video building GPT from zero
- [Let's build the GPT tokenizer](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zduSFxRajkE) — BPE tokenization from scratch
- [Deep Dive into LLMs](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7xTGNNLPyMI) — 3.5-hour intro to LLM internals
- [How I use LLMs](https://karpathy.bearblog.dev/how-i-use-llms/) — Practical usage patterns

---

### [Sebastian Raschka — Ahead of AI](https://magazine.sebastianraschka.com/)

Author of "Build a Large Language Model (From Scratch)". Weekly posts with hands-on code and clear explanations:

- [The State of LLMs 2025](https://magazine.sebastianraschka.com/p/state-of-llms-2025) — Year-in-review: reasoning models, inference scaling, architecture evolution
- [The Big LLM Architecture Comparison](https://magazine.sebastianraschka.com/p/the-big-llm-architecture-comparison) — Side-by-side comparison of GPT, Llama, Mistral, etc.
- [Understanding Large Language Models](https://magazine.sebastianraschka.com/p/understanding-large-language-models) — From self-attention to RLHF
- [LLM Training: RLHF and Its Alternatives](https://magazine.sebastianraschka.com/p/llm-training-rlhf-and-its-alternatives) — DPO, ORPO, and beyond
- [Tips for LLM Pretraining and Evaluating Reward Models](https://magazine.sebastianraschka.com/p/tips-for-llm-pretraining) — Practical pretraining guidance

---

### [Chip Huyen](https://huyenchip.com/blog/)

Expert on ML systems and production deployment. Essential for the engineering side:

- [Building a Machine Learning Platform](https://huyenchip.com/2024/07/25/genai-platform.html) — Designing production GenAI infrastructure
- [What I Learned from Looking at 900 ML Tools](https://huyenchip.com/2024/03/14/ai-oss.html) — Survey of the open-source ML landscape
- [Real-time Machine Learning](https://huyenchip.com/2022/01/02/real-time-machine-learning-challenges-and-solutions.html) — Deploying ML at scale

---

### [Hugging Face Blog](https://huggingface.co/blog)

Official blog from the open-source ML platform. Model releases, tutorials, and tools:

- [Open LLM Leaderboard](https://huggingface.co/spaces/open-llm-leaderboard/open_llm_leaderboard) — Benchmark rankings for open models
- [RLHF: Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback](https://huggingface.co/blog/rlhf) — Practical RLHF guide
- [Making LLMs even more accessible with bitsandbytes, 4-bit quantization and QLoRA](https://huggingface.co/blog/4bit-transformers-bitsandbytes) — Quantized fine-tuning
- [A Gentle Introduction to 8-bit Matrix Multiplication](https://huggingface.co/blog/hf-bitsandbytes-integration) — INT8 quantization explained
