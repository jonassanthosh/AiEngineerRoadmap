## Full Courses

### [Andrej Karpathy — Neural Networks: Zero to Hero](https://karpathy.ai/zero-to-hero.html)
The single best free course for understanding neural networks and LLMs from scratch. Karpathy builds everything from a basic autograd engine to a GPT implementation:

1. [The spelled-out intro to neural networks and backpropagation](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VMj-3S1tku0) — Build micrograd
2. [The spelled-out intro to language modeling](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PaCmpygFfXo) — Bigram models to MLPs
3. [Building makemore Part 2: MLP](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TCH_1BHY58I)
4. [Building makemore Part 3: Activations & Gradients, BatchNorm](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=P6sfmUTpUmc)
5. [Building makemore Part 4: Becoming a Backprop Ninja](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=q8SA3rM6ckI)
6. [Building makemore Part 5: Building a WaveNet](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t3YJ5hKiMQ0)
7. **[Let's build GPT: from scratch, in code, spelled out](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=kCc8FmEb1nY)** — The best GPT explainer video ever made
8. [Let's build the GPT Tokenizer](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zduSFxRajkE) — BPE from scratch
9. [Deep Dive into LLMs like ChatGPT](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7xTGNNLPyMI) — 3.5-hour overview of the full LLM stack
10. [How I use LLMs](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EWvNQjAaOHw) — Practical usage patterns

### [Stanford CS229 — Machine Learning](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLoROMvodv4rMiGQp3WXShtMGgzqpfVfbU)
Andrew Ng's legendary ML course. Covers linear regression through neural networks with rigorous math.

### [Stanford CS231n — Convolutional Neural Networks for Visual Recognition](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL3FW7Lu3i5JvHM8ljYj-zLfQRF3EO8sYv)
The classic deep learning course. CNNs, training tricks, architectures.

### [Stanford CS224n — NLP with Deep Learning](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLoROMvodv4rMFqRtEuo6SGjY4XbRIVRd4)
Chris Manning's NLP course. Word vectors through Transformers and pretraining.

### [Stanford CS25 — Transformers United](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLoROMvodv4rNiJRchCzutFw5ItR_Z27CM)
Guest lectures from researchers at OpenAI, Google, Meta on modern Transformer innovations.

---

### [MIT 6.S191 — Introduction to Deep Learning](http://introtodeeplearning.com/)
Fast-paced intro with TensorFlow/Keras labs. Covers fundamentals through generative models.

### [Fast.ai — Practical Deep Learning for Coders](https://course.fast.ai/)
Jeremy Howard's top-down approach. Build practical models first, understand theory later. Excellent for practitioners.

### [Hugging Face — NLP Course](https://huggingface.co/learn/nlp-course)
Free course covering the Transformers library, tokenizers, fine-tuning, and model deployment.

### [Hugging Face — How Transformer LLMs Work (with Jay Alammar)](https://huggingface.co/learn/llm-course)
Short, animated course on Transformer internals. Great companion to the illustrated blog posts.

## Individual Lectures & Talks

- **[3Blue1Brown — Neural Networks](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDNU6R1_67000Dx_ZCJB-3pi)** — The most beautiful visualizations of neural network math
- **[3Blue1Brown — Attention in Transformers, visually explained](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eMlx5fFNoYc)** — Visual attention mechanism explainer
- **[Yannic Kilcher's Paper Explainers](https://www.youtube.com/@YannicKilcher)** — Detailed video walkthroughs of ML papers
- **[Umar Jamil — Transformer/LLM Code Walkthroughs](https://www.youtube.com/@ulomarjamil)** — Line-by-line code explanations of attention, LoRA, quantization
- **[DeepLearning.AI Short Courses](https://www.deeplearning.ai/short-courses/)** — Free 1-hour courses on RAG, fine-tuning, agents (some with Andrew Ng)
